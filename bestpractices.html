Best Practices

Planting takes place in two ways: either naturally or artificially. The natural reforestation process relies on the rooting of seeds and sprouts from nearby trees without human assistance. But, natural reforestation is often insufficient and requires support through artificial reforestation.

There are two classification options for reforestation techniques, depending on the age of co-existing trees:

  • even-aged stands: mature overstory is removed to let the new stand grow;
  • uneven-aged stands: older and younger trees grow together.
